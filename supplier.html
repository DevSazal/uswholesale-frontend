Connect with Buyers

Premium Members are able to connect instantly with buyers to increase sales opportunities!

  • Become a Verified SupplierA Supplier Pass® is awarded to suppliers who have passed our authentication and verification process. Buyers can identify this elite group of suppliers by the Supplier Pass® icon displayed within their listings. Only Premium Members can apply for a Supplier Pass®.
  • View Buyer Contact InformationAs a Premium Member you are able to see the contact information of buyers who are searching and sourcing for products you carry. Having the ability to contact buyers directly provides an advantage over free members who must wait for a buyer to contact them.
  • Quote RFQs InstantlyAs a Premium Member you are able to quote RFQs and connect with buyers as soon as the RFQ is posted. Free members must wait 30 days in order to quote an RFQ posted by a buyer. This is a huge advantage to Premium Members, as most RFQs are fulfilled and orders placed within the first 30 days of being posted.
  • Exclusive Trade Show AccessPremium Members have exclusive access to Trade Shows where they can meet thousands of buyers who are looking for their products. TopTenWholesale produces trade shows in the U.S. and Canada under the Apparel Textile Sourcing brand.
